Standard Document Checklist

  • 1. Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • 2. Academic transcripts & degree certificates (attested)
  • 3. IELTS / PTE / Duolingo score report
  • 4. Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • 5. Two to three academic or professional reference letters
  • 6. Updated CV / resume
  • 7. Bank statements / financial sponsorship affidavit
  • 8. Passport-size photographs (as per embassy spec)
  • 9. Offer letter / CAS / I-20 (once issued)
  • 10. Visa application form & interview slot confirmation

Exact requirements vary by country and university — our documentation specialist confirms your personalised checklist during onboarding.
